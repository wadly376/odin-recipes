LasagNol

Chef Nol's ooey gooey lasagna will surely melt in your mouth. Cover your ears when serving because this dish will cause inexplicable deafening moans across the dinner table.

Ingredients

For the Sauce

  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • 2 chopped carrots
  • 1 chopped stalk celery
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 24oz of italian sausage
  • 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup of cheap red wine
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 3/2tsp dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves

For the Ricotta Mixture

  • 15oz whole milk ricotta
  • 3oz cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup of Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup chopped basil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • salt and black pepper to taste

For Assembling

  • 12 oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 16oz shredded whole milk mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup of Parmigiano-Reggiano

Making the Dish

  1. Place onions, carrots, and celery in a food processor and mince until fine but not puréed.
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil and cook and break apart sausage until slightly brown and fully separated; sauasage should be slightly undercooked. Transfer to bowl and set aside.
  3. In the pot, Sauté minced vegetables, mirepoix, until soft. Do not brown! Add in wine and stir scraping any bits from the bottom of the pot.
  4. Once wine is mostly absorbed, add in tomatoes, cream, salt, pepper, suagr, thyme, and bay leaves and bring to a boil. Bring to a simmer then reduce heat and allow sauce to thicken.
  5. Meanwhile, chop the sausage into small pieces and add back into pot once sauce is plenty thicc. Cook sauce for 10 minutes.
  6. After adding sausage to the pot, rinse out the food processor and combine ricotta, cream cheese, egg, garlic, parmigiano-reggiano, salt, and pepper. Blend until mixture is even. Add in basil and blend until basil is finely chopped.
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit. Grab deep baking dishh and spread sauce until bottom is covered. Place 3 noodles over the sauce then layer ricotta mixture on the noodles. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top of ricotta mixture. Repeat 3 more times then top with noodles.
  8. Spray tin foil with nonstick cooking spray and cover lasagna. Bake for 40 minutes then uncover and bake for another 20 minutes.
