Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients

  • Deep dish pie crust
  • 4 large eggs
  • 8oz of Pancetta
  • 4oz of Gruyère
  • 1/2cup of chopped shallots
  • 5/4cups of heavy cream
  • Nutmeg to taste
  • Cayenne pepper to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit and bake the crust in a pie dish until light golen-brown. If crust inflates, gently poke with a fork until air is expelled. Set aside and reduce oven temp to 325 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Cook pancetta until crisp and transfer to a plate leaving approximately 2 tablespoons of fat in the pan.
  3. Add shallots to pan and sauté in leftover fat until soft and translucent. Do not brown! Remove pan from heat and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, whisk eggs, heavy cream, salt, cayenne, and nutmeg until mixture is even

  6. Bake quiche for 30-45 minutes until egg mixture is set and slightly golden.
